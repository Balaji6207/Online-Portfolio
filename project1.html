Description

Sky Cinemas is a website for booking movie showtimes and seats. Customers can browse and book tickets for the latest releases, as well as purchase snacks and get promotions. Staff members have access to an staff portal to manage movies, promotions, and snack options.

My Role

I worked with a member in a group of five to manage the frontend development of the website. I worked on the customer portal, while my partner worked on the staff portal. After researching and getting inspirations from popular movie websites, we designed the workflow and UI of the website in Figma. Then, I had to translate the Figma design using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I learnt new techniques like creating an expand button, a drag-to-scroll function, and hover effects.
